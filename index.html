Things To See

Edakkal Caves

Located 1,200 meters above sea level,
Edakkal has two caves-one, which is 96 feet
long and 22 feet wide, and the other, 18 feet
long and 12 feet wide

Kuruva Island

Sprawling over 950 acres, Kuruva is a group of islands housing dense forests rich in flora and
fauna, most of which are rare in nature.

Chembra Peak

Located at an altitude of 2,000 meters, Chembra Peak is the highest peak in Wayanad. A natural heart-shaped lake that never dries up is the main highlight here.
